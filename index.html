17. Final Proposal

The Numbers, Plainly

What's included, what's optional, what's billed separately, and what it costs.

Scope

A complete custom frontend covering every page and section of the website.

Animation

2–3 signature custom GSAP animation moments (think the timeline, the map, or the introduction) with 3–4 animations included in total.

Media

2–3 media edits included where needed, such as the hero banner video or imagery.

SEO

Basic on-page SEO setup and search indexing configured by default, at no extra cost.

Structure & Scope

The final sitemap and site structure will be finalized directly with you, over WhatsApp or email, whichever is more convenient.

Floating Chat

A floating chat button (e.g., WhatsApp Business or a Live Chat widget) can be included on all pages of the site as an optional convenience for your visitors.

Timeline

The estimated timeline for this project is 25 to 30 days from the kickoff date, contingent on prompt feedback and asset delivery.

Support

Upon complete delivery of the website, we offer a 15-day support window. During this period, we will fix any technical bugs or issues that arise. However, please note that no new updates, modifications, or content uploads will be done under this support window; it is strictly dedicated to resolving post-launch technical issues.

Payment

The project billing is divided into three milestone payments:

• 50% Advance Payment: Required at kickoff to initiate the design and strategy phase.

• 25% Midway Payment: Due upon completion and approval of the design and initial layouts.

• 25% Final Payment: Due after complete website deployment and testing. Upon receipt of final payment, full open-source code handover and hosting server control will be transferred to the client.

Optimization

We guarantee complete cross-device and cross-browser compatibility. The website will be fully responsive and optimized to render flawlessly on all devices (mobile phones, tablets, and desktops) and major browsers (Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Edge). We ensure zero text overlaps, layout alignment discrepancies, or functional errors on any screen size.

Collaboration

To deliver within the 25–30 day schedule, the BNP Interiors team must be very prompt in providing content and feedback. We will require a designated, prompt contact person from your team to manage internal approvals and collaborate directly with us.

Requirements

The following assets and access are required from your team to initiate development:

1. Brand Identity: High-resolution logo files (preferably SVG/PNG) and brand guidelines (colors, fonts).

2. Media Assets: High-resolution images and videos of projects, organized by name.

3. Copywriting: Final or draft copy for the About Us, Services, Team, and Projects pages.

4. Technical Details: Login access to your domain registrar and hosting panel (if purchased), as well as SMTP server credentials and mail account details (necessary to route contact form enquiries directly to your inboxes).

5. Integrations: WhatsApp business number, social media profiles, and CRM credentials.

Backend (Optional)

The backend / CMS is optional. The frontend price covers the complete website on its own; the backend is a separate add-on if you'd like to manage content yourself. Having your own backend also means SEO indexing and basic setup are already configured for you by default.

Billed Separately

Any third-party service cost (for example, a paid Instagram feed plugin) is billed directly to and paid by you.

Hosting and domain charges are borne by the client. For backend storage, we default to free-tier options; if your needs exceed that limit, the upgrade is purchased by you.
